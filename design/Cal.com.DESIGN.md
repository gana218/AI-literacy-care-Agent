# Cal.com Design Specifications

This specification details the layout guidelines for dense metrics tables, charts, weekly activity widgets, and gamified progress elements.

## Metric Cards & Layout Density
- **Layout Grid**: 3-column structures for metrics, high information density with muted sub-labels.
- **Title Size**: Small uppercase tracking labels (e.g., `font-size: 11px`, `letter-spacing: 0.05em`, color: `#6E655F`).
- **Value Size**: Large bold tabular-nums format (e.g., `font-size: 24px`, color: `#2A2421`).

## Data Visualization (Recharts Style)
- **Line Stroke**: Sage Green (`#608066`) or Warm Amber (`#D97706`) - single solid lines only, no neons.
- **Area Fill**: Match stroke color, gradient fading to transparent at the bottom with 5-8% starting opacity.
- **Grid Lines**: Soft dashed lines (`strokeDasharray: "3 3"`, color: `#EFECE6`).
- **Tooltips**: Wrapped in small warm cream cards with thin borders and soft drop shadows.

## Gamification (Minimalist Badges & Calendar Heatmap)
- Elegant line-art badges with terracotta/sage green gradients.
- Strict calendar heatmap utilizing 5 shades of sage green (`#FAF6F0` for empty to `#48604D` for maximum read streak).
